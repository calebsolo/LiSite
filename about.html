Ligia "Li" Soileau, is a Licensed Clinical Social Worker and Board Approved Clinical Supervisor who has been practicing for 14 years. Her interest areas are working with individuals in life transition, families, and children. She specializes in treating grief, depression, anxiety, and trauma. Her belief is that intervention is a form of prevention and that recurring emotional and behavioral health issues limit our ability to experience peace. Her mission is to help clients cope with intense emotions and achieve goals that lead to greater fulfillment.

Li has a Master of Social Work from Louisiana State University and has worked in Baton Rouge, Denham Springs, and New Orleans. In addition to her work as a therapist she has a background in program development, nonprofit management, and board engagement. Li serves as a volunteer on the board of the Florida Parishes Human Services Authority and supervises Master of Social Work professionals working toward licensure in the state of Louisiana. She was born in Rivas, Nicaragua, grew up in the Greater New Orleans Area, and has lived in Baton Rouge since 2005.
